About Us

CIS Bakery was founded in class in the fall semester of 2018 by Taia, Trey, and Katelyn. We offer many bakery items such as cakes, cupcakes, and cookies. We are located in several different places in Iowa. Check out our locations page for more info on our locations. We are opened Monday through Saturday from 10 am to 6 pm.
